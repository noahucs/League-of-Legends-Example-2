Items in league of legends

Roles & Lanes

Popular Champions for each lane.

Below is a list of some of the most popular champions for each lane.

  • Top Lane: Darius, Garen, Fiora, Camille, Malphite

    • Top Lane is at the top of the Rift ad is usually filled with roles such as tanks and bruisers.

    Toplane Items

    • Sundered Sky
    • Sterak's Gage
    • Thornmail
  • Mid Lane: Ahri, Zed, Yasuo, LeBlanc, Orianna

    • The Mid Lane is in the center of the Rift and is often occupied by mages and assassins.

    Midlane Items

    • Rabadon's Deathcap
    • Seraph's Embrace
    • Mejai's Soulstealer
  • Bottom Lane (ADC): Jinx, Kai'Sa, Ezreal, Ashe, Miss Fortune

    • The Bottom Lane is at the bottom of the Rift and typically features a duo of an Attack Damage Carry (ADC) and a Support.

    ADC Items

    • Infinity Edge
    • Rapid Firecannon
    • Lord Dominik's Regards
  • Bottom Lane (Support): Thresh, Leona, Nami, Lulu, Morgana

    • Supports are champions that assist the ADC in the Bottom Lane by providing utility, crowd control, and protection.

    Support Items

    • World Atlas
    • Echoes of Helia
    • Locket of the Iron Solari
  • Jungle: Lee Sin, Kha'Zix, Elise, Warwick, Graves

    • Junglers are champions that roam the jungle area between the lanes, securing objectives and assisting their teammates.

    Jungle Items

    • Gustwalker hatchling
    • Mosstomper Seedling
    • Scorchclaw Pup
